About Us

We believe in transforming lives

The world's greatest problems - ranging from poverty, joblessness, crime and other similar issues - restrict access to crucial human needs. These needs include, but are not limited to, Access to Proper HealthCare, Access to Quality Education, Access to Food and Access to a source of Livelihood.

These and other problems are the motivations behind Trillion Chip Ltd. We believe in being devotees in solving the aforementioned problems. Our chief drive and agenda is to transform lives through Technology. Hence our slogan, “Transforming Lives”

Trillion Chip is an Umbrella Company holding 7 different companies with a skilled management board that is comprised of individuals of high regard and influence in the regional economic scene. However, most of the board members are at present anonymous - but can reveal their identities upon request.

Our Vision

To be the leading one-stop shop for live transformation through smart tech products and solutions

We believe that Technology is among the most powerful transformational tools of the 21st Century. We are leveraging it by developing smart market-driven products. We incorporate the latest trends in technology consumption to provide our users with utility, convenience and fun.

Our Mission

To transform lives through technology by designing products that transform businesses and individuals through market and consumer access.

Our mission is to transform lives by creating opportunity in the face of the world's problems. As such, our goals are in line with the UN Millenium Development Goals.
